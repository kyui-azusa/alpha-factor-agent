# 0007 — Share methods and process, not final alpha results

日期:2026-07-20
状态:已接受

Idea packets may share methods, pitfalls, visual explanations, failure cases, and abstracted factor hypotheses, but they must not expose complete reproducible alpha expressions, stock or ranking conclusions, final metric tables, raw 聚源 data, or sensitive field-level details. This keeps the social surface useful and credible without leaking the final answer or violating data boundaries.
