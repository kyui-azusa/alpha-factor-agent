# 0009 — Five-day compressed delivery line

日期:2026-07-20
状态:已接受

The five-day build will prioritize a real, tested M0-M3 trustworthy base: data contract, point-in-time merge, no-look-ahead tests, baseline factors, out-of-sample backtest, and report figures. M4-M5 can be lightweight or mocked where needed to demonstrate the structured LLM loop, while M6 must still produce defense and paper-ready materials rather than only social idea packets.

## Consequences

- Large-scale factor mining, complex neutralization, and full robustness studies are stretch goals.
- The paper and defense should be written around the trustworthy base, the closed-loop design, and selected reproducible examples.
- Social idea packets remain byproducts of real progress, not substitutes for the required final deliverables.
