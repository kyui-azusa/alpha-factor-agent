# 0005 — Idea packets are the main outward-facing artifact

日期:2026-07-20
状态:已接受

The platform's social value will come primarily from shareable idea packets for students outside the team, not from the internal feedback workflow. Team issue intake remains useful, but it is a convenience layer; the stronger proof of capability is repeatedly producing clear intermediate ideas from the research process that other groups want to read, reuse, or discuss.

## Consequences

- Static pages should prioritize idea packets, factor-method notes, visual snippets, and experiment prompts.
- The issue workflow should stay minimal unless it directly improves idea production or follow-up conversations.
- Social value ranks first in attention, but a deliverable result (defense bundle per ADR-0009/0010) remains a hard floor: priority reorders effort, it never cancels delivery.
